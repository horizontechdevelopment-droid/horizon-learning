# What Is Cybersecurity?

## Goal

Understand what cybersecurity means in plain English.

## Plain-English explanation

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computers, phones, networks, websites, accounts, and data from misuse or attack.

It includes learning how systems work, how they fail, and how to make them safer.

## Cybersecurity is not just hacking

Cybersecurity includes many areas:

- Protecting accounts
- Securing Wi-Fi
- Finding weak passwords
- Updating software
- Detecting suspicious activity
- Investigating incidents
- Testing systems with permission
- Teaching users how to avoid scams

## Red team and blue team

You may hear people talk about red team and blue team.

**Red team** means offensive security. These people test systems by thinking like attackers.

**Blue team** means defensive security. These people protect systems, monitor alerts, and respond to problems.

Both sides are important.

## Beginner focus

As a beginner, your first focus should be:

- Basic computer safety
- Linux basics
- Networking basics
- Safe tool usage
- Legal practice labs

## Stop and check

You are ready to continue when:

- [ ] You know cybersecurity is bigger than hacking
- [ ] You understand that permission matters
- [ ] You know both offense and defense exist
